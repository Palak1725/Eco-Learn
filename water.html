üí¶ How to Save Water at Home

  • Turn off taps while brushing teeth or washing hands.
  • Fix leaking taps and pipes immediately.
  • Collect leftover water from washing vegetables and reuse for watering plants.
  • Take shorter showers and avoid unnecessary water usage.
  • Use a bucket instead of hose for washing vehicles or cleaning floors.

üè° Build a Simple Rainwater Harvesting Prototype

  1. Take a plastic bottle and cut the top off to create a funnel.
  2. Place a piece of cloth at the opening to filter debris.
  3. Use a tray or small bucket to collect the water dripping from the bottle.
  4. Place it outside where rain can fall into it.
  5. Observe how water is collected and think how to use it.
