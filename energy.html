What is Renewable Energy?

Renewable energy comes from natural sources that never run out! The sun, wind, water, and even plants give us energy we can use safely. Unlike coal or oil, these sources don’t pollute the Earth.

How We Renew Energy

  • Solar Energy: Using sunlight to make electricity with solar panels.
  • Wind Energy: Using wind to turn big turbines and create power.
  • Hydropower: Using moving water in rivers to generate electricity.
  • Biomass: Using plants and waste to create fuel.

Build a Simple Home Prototype

You can make a mini solar-powered car at home!

  • Materials: Small solar panel, motor, wheels, cardboard base, tape.
  • Step 1: Attach the motor to the wheels.
  • Step 2: Connect the solar panel to the motor wires.
  • Step 3: Place the motor-wheel setup on the cardboard base.
  • Step 4: Put the solar car under sunlight and watch it move!
